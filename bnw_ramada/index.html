About BNW Ramada Residences

Ramada by BNW Developments introduces an exclusive residential project in Al Jaddaf, Dubai, featuring premium 1- and 2-bedroom apartments. Rising G+11 floors, the development blends modern architecture with elegance and functionality. Homes are designed with efficient layouts, expansive windows, and scenic views of Dubai Creek and Downtown. BNW Ramada Residences strategically located between Downtown and DIFC, Ramada BNW offers seamless connectivity via major highways, Metro, and upcoming Blue Line and Etihad Rail. With proximity to Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall, and vibrant leisure hubs, it ensures both lifestyle and investment appeal.
