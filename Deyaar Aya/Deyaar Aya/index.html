About Deyaar Aya

Aya by Deyaar redefines beachfront living in Umm Al Quwain, blending light, water, and innovation into a coastal landmark. Set within a flourishing community, its visionary architecture features undulating terraces, floor-to-ceiling windows, and breathtaking views of the Arabian Gulf and Al Khor mangroves. Deyaar Aya Residences is thoughtfully crafted with smart home technology, premium finishes, fitted kitchens, generous storage, and advanced sound and climate insulation. Deyaar Aya Beachfront Residences is inspired by the UAE’s coastal heritage, Aya Deyaar offers a refined waterfront lifestyle where contemporary luxuries and natural beauty harmonize, creating an unparalleled living experience by the sea.
